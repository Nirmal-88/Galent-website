01Forward-Deployed Engineering

AI Doesn't Fail Because of Models. It Fails Because of Missing Context.

PoCs are easy. Production is hard. The gap isn't model quality — it's the messy reality of enterprise systems, policies, and tribal knowledge that AI alone can't navigate.

Forward-Deployed Engineers close that gap. Senior practitioners embedded in your teams — not contractors, not consultants — who own the outcome end-to-end, backed by GalentAI at every step.

02 · Capabilities

What an FDE does.

A Forward-Deployed Engineer combines six capabilities that don't usually live in one role — and certainly don't usually live inside your team.

Owns outcomes, not tasks

Programme success is the unit of measurement. FDEs don't escalate, hand off, or wait — they finish.

→ End-to-end accountability

Reads the system, not just the ticket

Architecture, data flow, policy, dependencies — context is built before code is written. Tribal knowledge is captured, not assumed.

→ Context Graph-backed

Runs GalentAI in your environment

The platform plugs into your stack. The FDE runs the platform. You see velocity without changing how your team operates.

→ Embedded operation

Bridges business and engineering

Stakeholder conversations and architecture decisions sit in the same person. Translation cost approaches zero.

→ Single point of context

Builds, ships, observes

Code, infra, tests, observability, runbooks — all delivered together. No artificial team boundaries.

→ Full-stack delivery

Leaves behind a faster team

FDEs don't create lock-in. The platform, the context, and the patterns stay. Your team is faster after we leave.

→ Compounding handover
