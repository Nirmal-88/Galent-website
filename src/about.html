02Our Story

Built from First Principles. Not Assembled from Parts.

Most technology services companies were built to sell headcount. When AI arrived, they bolted copilots onto existing delivery models and called it transformation.

Galent was built differently. We started with the question: what would a technology services company look like if it were designed from scratch for the AI era?

The answer is GalentAI — an AI-native SDLC platform — and the Forward-Deployed Engineer model that operationalises it inside client environments.

We don't run pilots. We don't sell licences. We deliver outcomes — and the platform is what makes those outcomes possible at a speed and quality that no headcount model can match.

03What We Believe

The Principles Behind the Platform.

Production, not pilot

GalentAI was designed for production-grade deployment from day one. Not for impressive demos that collapse under real enterprise conditions.

Context is everything

The Achilles heel of AI is context window management. We built NeuroQL, the Knowledge Graph, and the Context Graph to solve it — permanently.

People own outcomes, platforms enable velocity

AI orchestrates the programme. Humans drive strategic decisions. This is not automation replacing engineering — it is engineering amplified.

One size fits one

Every enterprise is different. GalentAI adapts to your stack, your processes, your constraints — not the other way around.

Engineering empathy

The most valuable engineers in the AI era will understand systems before they solve them. This is the FDE philosophy — embedded, contextual, outcome-owned.

Compounding intelligence

Every engagement makes the platform smarter. Every decision captured. Every outcome institutionalised. Value compounds — it doesn't reset.
