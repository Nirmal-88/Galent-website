## Galent Announces Vijayakumar Anbu as Chief Architect of Galent AI Platform

![](/assets/images/uploads/galent-chief-ai-architect-vijayakumar-anbu-banner.png)

We are delighted to announce that Vijayakumar (Vijay) Anbu has joined Galent as the Chief Architect of the Galent AI Platform.

With over 20 years of experience across leading organizations including ConvergedHub, Hinduja Global Solutions, EPAM, HID, Ford Motor Company, IBM, and Fujitsu, Vijay brings a proven track record of designing highly scalable architectures, driving technical innovation, and delivering business-aligned solutions for Fortune 500 companies.

His expertise spans diverse industry verticals such as automotive, healthcare, and technology services, where he has successfully architected complex digital ecosystems, cloud migration initiatives, and cutting-edge AI-powered platforms. Vijay specializes in **RAG pipelines, AI orchestration, Multi-Agentic AI frameworks, and AI voice bot development**, enabling enterprises to harness the full potential of artificial intelligence.

A true technology leader, he excels at building synergy between technology and business stakeholders to ensure architectural excellence and measurable business outcomes. His deep architectural vision and passion for innovation will be instrumental in shaping the Galent AI Platform into a world-class ecosystem of intelligent, future-ready services.

Beyond his professional pursuits, Vijay is also an avid painter, cricketer, and traveler, bringing creativity and curiosity into everything he does.

Welcome aboard, Vijay – your vision will propel the Galent AI Platform into its next chapter of innovation.

## About Galent

Galent is an AI-native digital engineering firm, offering practical and business outcome-based services. It integrates digital engineering, workforce services and consulting into a unified, enterprise-ready solution.
