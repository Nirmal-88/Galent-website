A leading U.S.-based industrial parts distributor partnered with Galent to modernize its pricing portal central to its sales operations across MRO (Maintenance, Repair, and Operations) and OEM markets.

Built on a legacy monolithic stack, the platform had become increasingly difficult to scale, maintain, and enhance. Performance bottlenecks, tightly coupled components, and rising infrastructure costs limited the organization's ability to respond to evolving business demands and deliver a seamless user experience.

The engagement focused on transforming this legacy system using a structured modernization approach enabling incremental migration, improved performance, and future-ready scalability without disrupting ongoing business operations.

The result: a modern, flexible, and high-performance pricing platform designed for speed, resilience, and continuous evolution.

## Client Challenges

As the organization scaled, its pricing platform presented several structural and operational challenges:

**Legacy Monolithic Architecture:** The system, built on Java, ExtJS, and DB2, was tightly coupled with limiting agility and making even minor changes complex and risky.

**Performance Bottlenecks:** Heavy browser rendering and slow backend processing led to delayed pricing retrieval, directly impacting sales efficiency and user experience.

**High Infrastructure Costs:** The legacy architecture required significant infrastructure investment, with limited readiness for cloud-native scalability.

**Limited Agility & Time-to-Market:** Tightly integrated components slowed down release cycles, making it difficult to introduce new features or enhancements quickly.

**Risk of System-Wide Disruptions:** Lack of modularity meant that small updates could have cascading impacts across the entire system.

## Galent's Approach

Galent implemented a Modernize & Integrate strategy, combining incremental transformation with performance optimization and architectural redesign.

### Strangler Fig Modernization Strategy

Adopted the Strangler Fig pattern to enable gradual system transformation:

- Incremental replacement of legacy components
- Zero disruption to ongoing business operations
- Controlled migration with continuous validation

### Micro-Frontend Architecture

Introduced a modern Node.js and React.js-based micro-frontend architecture:

- Decoupled UI components for independent development and deployment
- Improved responsiveness and user experience
- Enhanced flexibility for future feature expansion

### Backend Integration & Data Strategy Evolution

Ensured seamless integration with existing systems while modernizing the data layer:

- Continued leveraging DB2 stored procedures for stability and continuity
- Integrated via Java JAR-based services for efficient communication
- Course-corrected data strategy mid-transformation to align with long-term goals
- Adopted MongoDB data models to enhance scalability, flexibility, and future readiness

### API-Driven, Modular Architecture

Built a robust backend with:

- Modular, reusable APIs for scalability
- Clear separation of concerns across services
- Improved maintainability and extensibility

### Enterprise-Grade Security & Quality Assurance

Embedded strong engineering practices:

- Enterprise-grade security frameworks
- Automated regression testing for reliability
- Continuous validation to ensure seamless releases

## Solution Delivered Execution Model

- Modernized pricing platform using Strangler Fig approach
- Node.js + React.js micro-frontend architecture
- Integration with legacy DB2 systems via Java services
- Modular API-driven backend
- Hybrid data strategy with MongoDB adoption
- Automated regression testing and secure deployment practices
- Incremental, zero-disruption modernization

## Business Impact

The modernization initiative delivered measurable improvements in performance, scalability, and operational efficiency.

### Key Outcomes

- **Improved Performance & User Experience:** Delivered significantly faster response times for pricing workflows enhancing sales productivity and customer experience.

- **Scalable Architecture for Growth:** Enabled horizontal scaling through microservices and modular components supporting future business expansion.

- **Reduced Maintenance Complexity:** Simplified deployments and reduced technical debt through decoupled architecture and modern frameworks.

- **Enhanced Agility & Faster Releases:** Enabled faster feature rollouts and updates through independent service deployment.

- **Future-Ready Platform:** Established a flexible foundation aligned with cloud-native and data modernization strategies.
