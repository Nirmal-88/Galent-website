## Galent Welcomes JinaPriya Ajitha Doss as Global Delivery Head – Services

![](/assets/images/uploads/jina-priya-global-delivery-head-galent-banner.png)

Galent announces that Jina Priya Ajitha Doss has joined the organization as Global Delivery Head – Services.

With over 24 years of experience in portfolio management, P&L ownership, and delivery leadership, Jina Priya brings expertise in scaling large portfolios, driving strategic growth, and delivering customer-centric outcomes for global clients. Her career spans multiple business ecosystems, where she has successfully led complex digital transformation programs, service modernization initiatives, and high-impact business growth strategies.

Known for building trusted relationships across organizations, Jina Priya is a proactive leader passionate about innovation and translating strategic conversations into measurable business results.

At Galent, Jina Priya will spearhead global delivery operations, driving operational excellence, AI-native delivery models, and future-ready service frameworks to enable transformative outcomes for clients. Her leadership strengthens Galent's commitment to innovation, client success, and delivery excellence.
