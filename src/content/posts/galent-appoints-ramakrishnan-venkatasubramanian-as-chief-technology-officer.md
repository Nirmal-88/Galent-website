### Galent Appoints Ramakrishnan Venkatasubramanian as Chief Technology Officer

**Galent is pleased to announce the appointment of Ramakrishnan Venkatasubramanian as its new Chief Technology Officer**

With over 25 years of global experience in digital transformation and enterprise-scale engineering, Ramakrishnan Venkatasubramanian brings deep expertise in building AI-native platform strategies that drive tangible business impact. His leadership spans Generative AI, Agentic AI, intelligent automation, cloud-native architecture, and emerging technologies like AR/VR, IOT, Web 3.0 positioning him at the forefront of next-gen enterprise innovation.

As CTO, he will lead Galent's technology vision and strategy driving innovation, enhancing digital experiences, and scaling AI-powered platforms for enterprise clients across industries.

Galent is excited to welcome a leader of Ram's caliber to the executive team.

Welcome aboard, Ram. The future of tech at Galent is in bold hands.
