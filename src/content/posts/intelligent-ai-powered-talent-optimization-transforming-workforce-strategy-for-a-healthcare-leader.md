### The Challenge: Future-Proofing Talent Acquisition

A U.S.-based digital transformation solutions company specializing in healthcare needed to modernize and streamline its talent supply chain across multiple global locations. With increasing competition for top-tier tech talent, the client sought a data-driven, AI-powered workforce strategy to:

- Enhance workforce scalability and agility.
- Optimize talent acquisition costs and time-to-hire.
- Improve demand forecasting and internal talent redeployment.
- Increase hiring quality through automation and analytics.

### The AI-Enabled Workforce Solution

Galent designed a comprehensive AI-driven workforce transformation model, leveraging predictive analytics, automation, and intelligent talent strategies to redefine hiring processes.

**Key Components of the Solution:**

- **AI-Powered Workforce Analytics:** Conducted an in-depth audit of existing hiring processes, benchmarking against industry best practices to identify gaps and opportunities for optimization.
- **Automated Talent Acquisition:** Implemented AI-driven sourcing, automated resume screening, and intelligent candidate matching to accelerate hiring.
- **GCC-in-a-Box:** Established a structured framework for global capability center (GCC) talent strategy, ensuring seamless international hiring and workforce expansion.
- **Machine Learning-Based Demand Forecasting:** Developed predictive models to anticipate hiring needs and align talent supply with business demand.
- **AI-Augmented Internal Redeployment:** Enabled automated skill-matching for internal mobility, optimizing workforce allocation and retention.
- **Process Automation for Hiring Optimization:** Integrated AI-powered interview scheduling, chatbots for post-offer follow-ups, and predictive analytics for offer conversion rate improvement.

### Impact Delivered

Through the AI-enabled workforce transformation, the client achieved measurable success:

- **20-25% Faster Hiring** across India and the U.S., reducing time-to-fill critical roles.
- **5-7% Cost Savings** through an optimized, variabilized spend model, reinvesting in strategic hiring initiatives.
- **Improved Hiring Quality** with AI-driven candidate evaluation and selection.
- **Reengineered Workforce Strategy** integrating process automation, enhanced organizational structures, and future-ready talent acquisition methodologies.
