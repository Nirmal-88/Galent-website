## Galent Unveils AI Studio in Chennai to Accelerate AI-Native Digital Engineering

![GalentAI Studio Opens in Chennai](/assets/images/uploads/galent-unveils-aistudio-in-chennai-to-accelerate-ai-native-digital-engineering-banner.png)

**Galent Unveils AI Studio in Chennai to Accelerate AI-Native Digital Engineering**

**Chennai, July 24 –** Galent, the AI-native digital engineering firm, today announced the opening of its new AI Studio at Global Infocity Park in Chennai. This state-of-the-art facility is designed to power Galent's next phase of growth, deepening its AI engineering capabilities and expanding its delivery footprint for global clients.

The studio was inaugurated by CTO Ramakrishnan Venkatasubramanian, CEO Ashwin Bharath, and Founder Sriram Rajagopal. Together, the leadership team underscored Galent's mission: to fuel next-gen enterprise transformation through AI-native engineering and local talent development.

"The GalentAI Studio is purpose-built to move fast, build smart, and solve real enterprise challenges – it's a creative crucible for building the future of enterprise AI," said CTO Ramakrishnan. "From Chennai, we aim to power global clients with intelligent applications, modern data architectures, and cloud-native platforms that learn, adapt, and scale."

## A New Frontier in AI-Native Engineering

Galent is at the forefront of the AI-native revolution, pioneering enterprise transformation through Second-Gen AI, Reverse Conway's Maneuver, and platform-driven service delivery. With a rapidly expanding footprint, Galent helps enterprises rewire their technology stacks for autonomy, scalability, and intelligence at every layer.

At the heart of this transformation lies the Galent AI Platform – a proprietary suite of modular tools that power the shift to AI-first organizations. From intelligent test automation and AI-augmented development to cloud modernization and enterprise-grade data infrastructure, the platform will enable scalable, adaptive, and intelligent enterprise solutions.

"As enterprises race to operationalize GenAI, we're building the systems and environments to help them scale with confidence," said CEO Ashwin Bharath. "This studio is a signal of that intent – and a milestone in our journey to outpace, outplay, and outstand."
