### From Complexity to Clarity: AI's Role in Modernizing Asset Servicing

A global asset servicing leader faced challenges in scaling operations, ensuring compliance, and enhancing customer engagement. Galent's AI-powered solutions helped the firm implement automated reporting, compliance monitoring, and advanced market analysis to drive operational efficiency, reduce risk, and deliver personalized services. The result: improved decision-making, fraud detection, and compliance accuracy while streamlining operations and enhancing client satisfaction.

## Client Challenges

**Complex Decisioning for Lending and Risk Management:**
Manual, data-heavy processes slowed decision-making and increased the potential for human error.

**Fraud Detection Gaps:**
Fragmented data sources and lack of predictive insights hindered fraud prevention efforts.

**Compliance Pressure:**
Stringent regulatory requirements required real-time monitoring and reporting, which strained resources.

**Operational Inefficiencies:**
Labor-intensive workflows led to delayed service delivery and limited scalability.

## Strategic AI Interventions

* **Automated Report Generation and Compliance Monitoring:** Leveraged AI to streamline compliance processes, automate reporting, and ensure regulatory adherence.

  **Outcome:** Achieved real-time compliance tracking and reduced manual reporting efforts by 50%.

* **Risk Management and Fraud Detection:** Implemented AI-driven risk modeling and fraud detection tools to analyze patterns and predict threats.

  **Outcome:** Enhanced fraud detection rates by 45% and minimized false positives through real-time data analysis.

* **Personalized Services Through Digital Identities:** Built an AI-powered framework for creating and managing digital identities, enabling personalized client offers and interactions.

  **Outcome:** Delivered tailored services, improving client satisfaction and loyalty.

* **Process Automation and Emergency IVR:** Introduced intelligent process automation to optimize workflows and deployed an AI-powered emergency IVR for faster issue resolution.

  **Outcome:** Reduced operational bottlenecks, improving scalability and response times.

## Key Outcomes

1. **Enhanced Operational Efficiency:** Achieved real-time compliance tracking and reduced manual reporting efforts by 50%.

2. **Improved Risk Mitigation:** Enhanced fraud detection rates by 45% and minimized false positives through real-time data analysis.

3. **Data-Driven Decision-Making:** Predictive insights empowered the firm to make strategic lending and investment decisions.

Galent's AI-powered solutions transformed the client's operations and strengthened its position as an innovator in the asset servicing industry.
